We are experienced in shoe repairs, mostly heels, lifts, soles, and stretching. But we can do other small fixes as well. Contact us for repairs and let us know your problem and which offered materials you want. We now customize shoes to, contact if interested!

SOLES & HEELS

The main reason some people prefer full soles & heels is appearance, but when worn-down, not only do they look awful, they may cause strains and pains in your ankles, legs, and back. They need to be replaced when holes appear, the old soles soften at the points of greatest wear, or you begin to feel the ground. We use replacement heels made of rubber, leather, or a combination of both materials. To keep your shoes looking good, we recommend leather or combo heels. When resoling shoes, you can choose between synthetic and leather. In general, if you’re paying to resole expensive shoes, you’ll probably want to pay a little more for leather since it’s more flexible and porous, which allows your tootsies to breathe and not get too sweaty or hot.

STRETCHING

We can make shoes fit better. We can stretch a pair that's too tight, add some space for toes, or raise an instep. We can also make footwear comfier and wear better with a range of tricks and add ons, including heel cushions and insoles. Just tell us where your shoe is rubbing, where it isn’t fitting, and what your problem is. We can suggest solutions.
